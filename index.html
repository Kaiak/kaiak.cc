Kaiak Nano Wallet

A Nano wallet for the KaiOS family of phones! Also usable directly in the browser to the right! ;-)

Features

  • Seed encrypted securely on device
  • Create wallet and receive / send Nano
  • Send by QR code
  • Make alias for your accounts
  • Export seed

Supported languages

  • English
  • Hindi
  • Spanish
  • Dutch
